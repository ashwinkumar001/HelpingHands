 About Us

We are a non-profit organization & working for social development & Our mission to provide free education to all and enhance their skills to make them capable to earn. We want to work on this project on pan India level. We are also working to provide them daily deeds on weekly basis with the help of public support. People can also help us by donating the needful things like food (in packed condition), clothes, footwear, toys, books & stationery items etc. whatever you want to donate. We will distribute these things to needful people and doner can also join us for distribution. We want more public/volunteer support too.

 

     OUR STORY

The journey of Forever Foundation is not too long. Its just have simple and pure intentions. The idea of this organization is not planed and not discussed with anyone. This is a result of a simple question "What should I need to do with a certain amount which I was going to donate in the name of GOD form my salary?" So this idea was grown up from there and I start to use that amount for social causes & charity. The word "Forever" I love most & it's personally connected to me so I used it and then the journey begins to spread the smile everywhere.
