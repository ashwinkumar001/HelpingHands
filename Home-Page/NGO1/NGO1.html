Food
We are providing free food
for needful people who
don't have the money or
sources. Our intention is
just that to help them,
not to make them addicted
for this. After some time
we'll try to make them
capable to earn something
for him/her or their
family too.

Clothes & Shoes
This is also important for
all of us. We will try to
distribute clothes & shoes
in sufficient amount. Our
supporters like you will
provide us with all these
required things on regular
basis as per the
availability. Our members
will collect this donation
from several locations or
you can send us on our
given address via courier
or drop at our place by
yourself.

Education
Don't think that the
education belongs to
money. We are working on
a model to set a module
for free education for
all at NO COST for them.
Our aim to provide free
education to make them
literate. Once they become
educated our foundation
will help them to start
earning. This will make
them feel proud and
capable to earn something
through their education
and other skills.

Message to everyone...
This organization is completely non- profit organization. Our message for all "Your Help is Hope of someone". We start this initiative to make smile on everyone faces. We are providing free food, clothes, shoes & our mission to provide free education to all and enhance their skills to make them capable to earn. Our foundation is following some fundamentals like we are not taking any kind of donation or help in form of CASH from any person/company/political-party etc. We have the only agenda that to make smile on the maximum number of the face with the pure intention of charity. We are here to help people with your support and love.
